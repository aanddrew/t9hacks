Welcome to sendgame. This game is a text-based dungeon-crawler.

You play by navigating your way through a maze of rooms.
In each room you may find treasure or an enemy, or both!

You interact with the game by sending it commands.
These commands are as follows
"move [direction]"" where direction is either east, west, north south.
"move" without any direction will display what directions you can move
"attack" attacks the enemy in the room
"treasure" will open the treasure chest in the room
"stats" to display stats
